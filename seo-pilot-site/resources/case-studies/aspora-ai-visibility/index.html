Aspora — From Rankings to AI Visibility

Client: aspora.com

The Challenge

Aspora needed to grow organic visibility and establish authority in search. Goals included scaling impressions and clicks, improving content structure for both traditional SEO and emerging AI answer engines.

The Strategy

We focused on intent-aligned content, strategic internal linking, and clear topical signals. Work included keyword and competitor research, content briefs, production, and optimization—plus structuring content so it could be cited reliably by AI systems.

The Results

Over a 6‑month period, Aspora saw strong growth in Google Search Console:

  • 7.49M impressions
  • 89.1K clicks
  • Clear upward trend in both metrics from mid–late 2025 into early 2026.

Below: search performance over the 6‑month window (clicks and impressions).

Why AI Started Citing Them

We structured Aspora’s content around clear entities, answer-friendly sections, and reliable sourcing. That made it easier for tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini to reference and cite their pages when users ask relevant questions—extending reach beyond traditional search.
